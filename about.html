I'm a staff sports photographer for PA Media (formerly the Press Association)

Before completing my NCTJ Press Photography at Norton College, I was awarded a First Class Honours degree in photography from Sheffield Hallam University.

I've worked for PA since 2012 and cover all kinds of sport, all over the world
